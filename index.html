Learn About Some Common Diseases

diabetes

Diabetes is a chronic disease that occurs when the body cannot properly regulate blood sugar levels. It can be caused by insufficient insulin production or poor insulin use. Symptoms may include excessive thirst, frequent urination, and tiredness. A healthy diet, regular exercise, and medical treatment are essential to manage diabetes.

covid-19 vaccine

COVID-19 is an infectious disease caused by a coronavirus. It spreads mainly through respiratory droplets when an infected person coughs, sneezes, or talks. Common symptoms include fever, cough, fatigue, and difficulty breathing. Vaccination, good hygiene, and wearing masks in crowded places help reduce the spread of the virus.

prevent epidemic

Preventing epidemics requires early detection, public awareness, and strong healthcare systems. Regular handwashing, vaccination, safe food practices, and rapid response to outbreaks help limit the spread of infectious diseases. Community cooperation plays a key role in protecting public health.
